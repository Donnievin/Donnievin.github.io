This predominantly follows the [A First Course in Bayesian Statistical Methods by Peter D. Hoff]([https://www.stat.berkeley.edu/~rabbee/s154/ISLR_First_Printing.pdf](https://sites.math.rutgers.edu/~zeilberg/EM20/Hoff.pdf)). All datasets to rerun the necessary calculations can be found [here](https://pdhoff.github.io/book/)

All rights belong to their rightful owners.

Topics: 

1. Belief, Probability, and exchangeability
2. One parameter models
3. Monte Carlo approximation
4. The Normal model
5. Posterior approximation with Gibbs sampler
6. The Multivariate normal model
7. Hierarchical modeling
8. Lienar Regression
9. Metropolis-Hastings Algorithms
10. Linear and Generalized Mixed Linear Effects models
11. Latent variable methods for ordinal data
